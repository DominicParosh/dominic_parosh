Weight Estimation for structural load analysis

This project pioneers real-time load monitoring for buildings and bridges, utilizing advanced Computer Vision and Video Annotation libraries. Employing object detection and tracking algorithms, the system analyzes video feeds to estimate the weight of vehicles entering and exiting structures. Machine learning models enhance accuracy by predicting weights based on size and shape. The integration of video annotating libraries labels key elements, facilitating precise calculations. In continuous real-time monitoring, the system issues alerts if the cumulative load approaches or surpasses safety thresholds. The collected data supports historical analysis, contributing to long-term structural optimization and safety.

Land mine Classification

Detection of mines buried in the ground is very important in terms of safety of life and property. Many different methods have been used in this regard; however, it has not yet been possible to achieve 100% success. Mine detection process consists of sensor design, data analysis and decision algorithm phases. The magnetic anomaly method works according to the principle of measuring the anomalies resulting from the object in the magnetic field that disturbs the structure of it, the magnetic field, and the data obtained at this point are used to determine the conditions such as motion and position. The determination of parameters such as position, depth or direction of motion using magnetic anomaly has been carried out since 1970.

Wine Quality Predictor

This project centers around the 'winequality-red' dataset, encompassing detailed information on 1599 different red wine brands and their corresponding quality ratings. The dataset includes various factors such as fixed acidity, volatile acidity, citric acid content, residual sugars, chlorides, free and total sulfur dioxide levels, density, pH value, sulphates, and alcohol percentage. Each of these elements contributes to determining the quality of the red wine. Through comprehensive analysis and modeling, this dataset serves as a valuable resource for understanding the intricate relationships between these factors and the overall quality of red wine, providing insights into the key determinants of wine excellence.
